Our principles are fundamental to success. They focus on how we strengthen, secure, and develop our company over time. Adhering to these principles is how we will achieve our vision, "To create share value added to all customers and establish our position as the main supplier for Modified Tapioca Starch products" . We cannot guarantee positive outcomes or risk-free results, from time to time, we may fall shorts in our efforts, and if that happens, we will renew our commitment to these principles, and re-double our efforts. What we can promise is to be truthful and provide honest assessment of our business and prospects. We will operate with resolve to make the organization in which our clients, employees, shareholders, and communities can be proud of.
