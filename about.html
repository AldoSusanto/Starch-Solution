Pt. Starch Solution International was established in early 1996 as a subsidiary of AVEBE UA, Netherlands. With over 20 years of experience in Modified Tapioca Starch industry, our organisation has successfully maintained its position as the largest Modified Tapioca Starch producer in Indonesia. Additionally, our products have been distributed worldwide, having strong market presence in the South East Asian market namely, Japan, China, Korea Philippines, Turkey, India, Malaysia, Netherlands, New Zealand, and many more.

At Pt. Starch Solution International, we are committed in providing first-rate quality products with reliable consistency. Through both advanced technologies and rigorous supplier audits, we ensure that our products are manufactured to the highest quality and meet international standards. Moreover, our outstanding Quality Control team maintain consistent quality throughout our product range by carrying out meticulous quality control procedure to eliminate product defects entirely before leaving the production line.

Consequently, we recognise the significance of Sustainable Development; this means adopting business strategies and activities which comply with meeting the needs of our stakeholders while simultaneously protecting, sustaining, and contributing positively to social welfare, and the environment.

Our product, tapioca Starch is commonly applied to paper, textile, ceramics, and many other industries. The quality and consistency of products could be reflected by our award, ISO 9001: 2015 (Design and Manufacture of Modified Starch) which has significant role in our development.
