OUR STRATEGY

QUALITY

We believe in manufacturing products that are of the highest quality. By utilising our advanced European technology equipment, we are capable of processing raw materials efficiently whilst maintaining its quality. Additionally, we ensure the quality of our raw materials through our periodic and rigorous supplier audits.

CONSISTENCY

Our Quality Control team is the pride of our company. Achieving ISO 9001:2015, our zero defects Total Quality Management system coupled with exceptional performance by our team members have resulted in steady productions with exceptionally few defects quantity.

SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT

We understand that people are core to our organisation, at Starch Solution International, we provide numerous incentives namely, competitive salaries, profit sharing, and industry leading benefit to maintain engagement in their work. Furthermore, our products are manufactured from botanical resources, and recognised as natural polymers which are friendly towards the environment.

SATISFACTION

We believe it is of the utmost importance that the needs and expectations of our clients are fulfilled through correct application of quality management system to assure highest degree of customer satisfaction. Going the extra ‘mile’ for our clients have been our rule of thumb.
